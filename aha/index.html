Frequently Asked Questions

How does Inomy make money without ads?

We earn a small commission when you purchase through our platform, similar to cashback services. Instead of keeping it all, we share this value with you through IXP rewards.

What makes Inomy different from Google Shopping?

Google Shopping shows sponsored results first. We use neutral AI that doesn't favor any seller - just finds the best match for your needs. Plus, you earn rewards instead of being the product.

Do I need to sign up to try it?

No! You can try your first few searches completely free without any account. We only ask you to sign in when you want to earn and save IXP rewards.

How quickly can I redeem my IXP points?

Most active users reach their first redemption within a week. Win leaderboard positions to unlock gift cards from Amazon, Starbucks, Target, and more.

Is my shopping data private and secure?

Absolutely. We never sell your data to advertisers. Your shopping preferences help train our AI to serve you better, but your personal information stays completely private.
