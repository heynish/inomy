Frequently Asked Questions

How does Inomy make money without ads?

We earn a small commission when you purchase through our platform, similar to cashback services. Instead of keeping it all, we share this value with you through IXP rewards. No ads, no bias, just transparent value sharing.

What makes Inomy different from Google Shopping?

Google Shopping shows sponsored results first. We use neutral AI that doesn't favor any seller - just finds the best match for your needs. Plus, you earn rewards for every search instead of being the product.

How quickly can I redeem my IXP points?

You can redeem points starting at 1,000 IXP ($10 value). Most users reach this within their first week of active shopping. Redemptions are processed within 24-48 hours.

Is my shopping data private and secure?

Absolutely. We never sell your data to advertisers. Your shopping preferences help train our AI to serve you better, but your personal information stays private. Built on privacy-first principles.

Which stores and categories are supported?

We currently support electronics, home appliances, and tech gadgets from major retailers like Amazon, Best Buy, Target, and 500+ other stores. More categories launching soon.

How accurate are the AI recommendations?

Our AI achieves 94% user satisfaction in finding the right product match. It understands context, budget constraints, and personal preferences to deliver highly relevant results without algorithmic bias.
